COVID Out Break Maps

The COVID Out break maps allow users to see the distrobution of COVID-19 and its effect intems of te following

  • Total Deaths For each country
  • Total Cases For each country
  • Mortality For each country

These various fields can be interactively displayed using the layer control button in the top right hand corner of the window.

COVID AI

COVID AI allows uses to specifiy any date in the past or furture along with a country name. COVID AI will then issue a prediction of cumulative deaths and cases for that given county for the entered date.

NOTE: These predictions are based on numerical modeling of the situation and although we hope they are informative they only act as our best estimate.
